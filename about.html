American Hospital's Mission


American Hospital is committed to the health and wellness of our community.
It’s an extensive network of our staffs with high skills, caring professionals who are dedicated to the health and wellness of the communities they serve. As such, we dedicate ourselves to being a center of excellence in providing high-quality health care. We pledge compassionate support, personal guidance, and uncompromising standards to our patients in their journeys toward the health of body and mind. To ensure innovative and unsurpassed care for our patients, we are dedicated to maintaining our position as regional leaders in select medical specialties. And to enhance the wellness of our community, we commit ourselves to provide a diverse array of educational and outreach programs.


Specifically, the American Hospital system includes: Our hospitals located in Tirana, Fier, and Prishtina. In our hospitals have nearly 160 physicians on staff and more than 1,400 employees. American Hospitals have nearly 380 patient encounters annually. Over 317,100 patients have received services from the American Hospital since the inauguration of the first branch until today. All American hospitals are full-service, acute-care facilities that offer particular expertise in maternity services, women’s health, cancer care, sports medicine, cardiovascular, and surgery services.

History

American Hospital was opened in 2007 as the first private hospital in Tirana, Albania. The origin facility “Ballkan Alliance” was opened in 2006 and had only 16 beds. This health center was founded by SEAF whose partners were from Europe and the USA. The organization SEAF managed to invest in this hospital to improve medical, telecommunications, and financial services.

In the beginning, our hospital was focused on Cardiology and Cardiovascular Surgery and the doctors from Ankara and Istambul made these surgeries successful. From their experiences and knowledge, the Albanian doctors took these doctors’ masterpieces to improve our Health Care better. In November 2007, doctors and medicates have done the first transplant of kidneys and from that successful surgery made this hospital to go bigger and bigger. They did not only invested in this hospital but they also invested in General Surgery, aesthetics surgeries, gastroenterology, otorhinolaryngology, orthopedics, oncology, ophthalmology and, advanced radiology. Moreover, they archived to ensure the Coronarography, Coronary Artery Revascularization, and Electrophysiology apertures. What more important is that they could bring the best specialist from the best-developed countries to help and intervene in operations successfully. From their greatest achievements, it was possible to make sure that the transports and interventions are highly best and fast to help these medical specialists to save people‘s life. One of the achievements of the cardiologists of this hospital was realizing the Revascularization of Coronary arteries in an acute condition of myocardial infarctions for the first time in Albania.
